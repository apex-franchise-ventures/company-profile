01 — About

A diversified house of ventures.

APEX Ventures, established in 2018, is a sister company of Mineski Global — one of Southeast Asia's most recognized pioneers in esports, gaming, and technology.

Built on the same spirit of innovation and forward-thinking, we have grown into a trusted all-in-one partner — delivering affordable, innovative, and sustainable solutions across e-commerce, trading, distribution, ICT, event production, and end-to-end booth fabrication.

Guided by integrity and long-term vision, we leverage modern platforms and digital tools to make access faster, more convenient, and built to last.
